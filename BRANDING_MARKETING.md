- AI-powered speaking practice (not just scripted dialogues)
- Live expert sessions (not just self-paced)
- Comprehensive exam prep (not just general English)

**vs. Rosetta Stone**:
- Modern, mobile-first design (not outdated interface)
- AI conversation partners (not just speech recognition)
- Flexible learning paths (not rigid curriculum)

**vs. iTalki/Preply**:
- Structured curriculum (not just tutoring)

- More affordable (unlimited AI practice vs. $10-30/hour)
